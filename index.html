Stinky Tanuki

After playing in the mud all day, Tanuki is very stinky. You must give her a good scrub before bedtime. Unfortunately for you, however, Tanuki HATES getting cleaned. She will run away every chance she gets, but it is your goal to make sure she's clean before night time rolls around.
In every difficulty of the game, you must click on Tanuki to scrub her, but the different difficulties determine how much energy she has, how dirty she is, and also how much time you have left before bed time. All these info will be displayed on the top left as you play.

Caution! This game has fast moving images. Please play at your own risk.
